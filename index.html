I am a Professor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at University of Minnesota. I received my PhD from the School of Industrial and Systems Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Renato Monteiro and Arkadi Nemirovski.

 

Research Interests
  • Theory and algorithms for deterministic and stochastic continuous optimization
  • Applications in data science, machine learning, statistics, finance, image sciences, and signal processing
